## 🚀 Overview

**AQUA** is a full-stack, AI-driven smart aquaculture system designed to help farmers and stakeholders:

- Monitor **live farm conditions**
- Predict **yield, risk, and readiness**
- Optimize **resources and profitability**
- Connect to **local & global markets**

The platform blends **real-time data**, **AI predictions**, and **clean system design** into a single intelligent dashboard.

---

## 🧠 Key Capabilities

### 🔴 Live Farm Intelligence
- Real-time water quality monitoring
- Location-aware farm analytics
- Biological health & growth tracking

### 📊 Water Quality Analytics
- 🌡️ Temperature
- 🧪 pH level
- 💧 Dissolved Oxygen (DO)
- 🌫️ Turbidity
- 🧂 Salinity

### 🧬 Biological Tracker
- Stock health index
- Growth rate estimation
- Harvest readiness prediction

### ⚠️ Risk Center
- Oxygen crash probability
- Disease risk estimation
- Weather-based alerts
- Stability indicators

### 🤖 AI Command Insights
- AI-powered feeding suggestions
- Equipment recommendations
- Power usage estimation
- Predictive system insights

### 🌍 Market Intelligence
- Live local fish prices
- Demand-based pricing signals
- Market trend visibility
