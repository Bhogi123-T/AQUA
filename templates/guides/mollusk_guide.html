🐚 Mollusk & Seaweed Farming

Integrated Multi-Trophic Aquaculture (IMTA) involving mussels, oysters, and seaweeds is the most sustainable way to farm the ocean.

Optimal Environment

  • 🌡️ Temperature: 15°C - 25°C (Species dependent)
  • 🌊 Water Flow: Moderate currents for nutrient supply
  • ⚓ Substrate: Ropes, rafts, or cages
  • 🌱 Nutrients: Nitrogen-rich waters for seaweed

Key Success Factors

Site Selection

Choose sites away from industrial runoff to ensure metal-free harvests.

Density Control

Avoid overcrowding to prevent nutrient depletion and stunted growth.

Harvest Timing

Harvest seaweed before high-temperature cycles to avoid bleaching.
