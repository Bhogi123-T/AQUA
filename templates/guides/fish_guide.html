🐟 Freshwater Fish Management

Carp species like Rohu, Catla, and Mrigal are the backbone of freshwater aquaculture. They are robust, highly nutritious, and have excellent market demand.

Optimal Parameters

  • 🌡️ Temperature: 25°C - 30°C
  • ⚖️ pH: 7.0 - 8.5
  • 🧂 Salinity: 0 - 5 ppt (Freshwater)
  • 🌬️ Dissolved Oxygen: > 3.0 mg/L
  • 🧪 Ammonia: < 0.2 mg/L

Key Success Factors

Polyculture

Grow Rohu, Catla, and Mrigal together to utilize different pond layers efficiently.

Natural Feed

Promote plankton growth using organic manures like cow dung or poultry waste.

Water Quality

Monitor turbidity and ammonia levels weekly to ensure a stress-free environment.
