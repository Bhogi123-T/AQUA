🦀 Mud Crab Culture Guide

Mud Crab farming is one of the most profitable aquaculture ventures. It can be done as 'Fattening' (quick turnaround) or 'Grow-out' (full culture).

Optimal Parameters

  • 🌡️ Temperature: 26°C - 30°C
  • ⚖️ pH: 7.5 - 8.5
  • 🧂 Salinity: 15 - 30 ppt
  • 🌬️ Dissolved Oxygen: > 3.0 mg/L
  • 🌊 Water Source: Brackish or Marine

Key Success Factors

Fence Management

Crabs are escape artists. Use sturdy nylon net or bamboo fencing around the pond.

Shelter Design

Provide hiding spots using PVC pipes or tiles to reduce cannibalism after molting.

Feeding

Feed trash fish or pellets at 5-10% of body weight, preferably during late evening.
