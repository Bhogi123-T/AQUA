Welcome to the future of aquaculture. Starting a farm today requires a blend of traditional wisdom and cutting-edge AI. This guide will walk you through the essential steps to launch your successful aquaculture venture.

1. Site Selection & Water Source

Choosing the right location is 70% of the success. Use our Location Advisor to check if your soil and climate are suitable for your target species.

2. Pond Construction

Ensure your pond is designed for optimal drainage and aeration. For shrimp farming, HDPE liners are highly recommended to maintain water quality and prevent soil-borne diseases.

3. Seed Selection

Always source seeds from SPF (Specific Pathogen Free) hatcheries. Use our Seed Checker to verify quality markers before stocking.

4. Real-time Monitoring

Install IoT sensors for pH, DO, and Temperature. Link them to your AquaSphere dashboard for 24/7 AI-driven monitoring and early warning alerts.
